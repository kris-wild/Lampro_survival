Toolbox: popDyn

A collection of routines for stepping from individuals to populations with different book-keeping schemes.

sgr, ssd, fis0

Specific population growth rate (sgr), population statistics (ssd) and f for which sgr=0 (fis0).

SSM

Semi Structured Model for populations in a generalized reactor, starting from a single freshly-laid egg. Embryoa, juveniles and adults are delineated, but without an internal structure.

cpm

Cohort Projection Model for populations with synchronized periodic reproduction events in a generalized reactor, starting from a single freshly-laid egg.

ebt

Escalator Boxcar Train for populations in a generalized reactor, starting from a single freshly-laid egg.
