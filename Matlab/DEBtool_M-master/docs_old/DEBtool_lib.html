Toolbox: lib

General purpose routines are parked in this domain. Most of them are called from sister directories (domains), but the routines mentioned on this manual page can be used independently, after setting the correct path to this subdirectory.

The sub domains are

  • regr regression routines
  • prob probability calculus
  • misc miscellaneous routines
  • pet add_my_pet routines
